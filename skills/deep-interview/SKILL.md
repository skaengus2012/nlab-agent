# Deep Interview

## Purpose
Reduce ambiguity before starting work. This is especially useful for PMs defining new features.

## Process
1. **Initial Scoring**: Assess the ambiguity of the user's request (0.0 to 1.0).
2. **Socratic Questions**: Ask 1-2 targeted questions per round to clarify:
   - Goals (What are we really trying to do?)
   - Constraints (What are the limitations?)
   - Success Criteria (How do we know it's done?)
3. **Synthesis**: Create a clear specification once ambiguity is low enough (typically < 0.2).

## Guidelines
- Limit rounds to 3-5 to avoid "interview fatigue".
- Focus on the weakest dimension (most ambiguous part) first.
- Always provide a summary of what has been clarified so far.
